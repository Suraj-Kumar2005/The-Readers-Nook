The Secret

A transformative guide to the law of attraction, revealing how positive thoughts and affirmations can shape wealth, health, and happiness. This inspiring work encourages readers to harness their mindset to manifest desired outcomes, offering a hopeful vision for personal growth and empowerment. It motivates transformative change despite ongoing debates over its scientific basis.-> perfect for readers of all age groups.

Will You Still Love Me?

A poignant romance, Will You Still Love Me? follows Lavanya, an Assamese girl, and her Punjabi partner as they navigate love and cultural differences. Set in vibrant Patiala and serene Meghalaya, Ravinder Singh’s evocative tale explores societal taboos and acceptance, delivering a heartfelt story of love’s resilience.-> perfect for readers of 16-26 years.

Revolution 2020

A gripping love triangle between Gopal, Raghav, and Aarti unfolds against India’s corrupt educational system. Gopal, from a poor background, and wealthier Raghav compete for Aarti while pursuing their ambitions. The incisive narrative exposes coaching institute exploitation and political corruption, weaving moral dilemmas into a vivid portrait of modern India. -> perfect for readers of 16-20 years.
